The Main Section

This is my content that talks about styling text. This text will transform through the various stages of displaying the font-family, padding, text-align, font-size, line-height, and letter-spacing CSS properties. At first, the text will appear okay. It most likely will not be very aesthetically pleasing, but it will accomplish its job of communicating a message to the visitor. However, just because your text is doing its job, that doesn't mean it can't do its job better.
